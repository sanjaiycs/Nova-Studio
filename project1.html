Brand Identity Design

Branding

Overview

A comprehensive brand identity project that transformed a local business into a market leader. We developed a complete visual identity system including logo design, color palette, typography, and brand guidelines.

The client needed a complete brand overhaul to better reflect their values and connect with their target audience. Their existing identity was outdated and inconsistent across different platforms.
